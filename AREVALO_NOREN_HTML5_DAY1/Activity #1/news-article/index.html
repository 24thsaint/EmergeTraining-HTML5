Congress to seek authority to license, regulate casinos

ABS-CBN News Posted at Jun 08 2017 09:38 AM

MANILA - House Majority Leader Rodolfo Fariñas said Thursday he would file a bill to transfer the power to regulate and grant licenses to casinos to Congress from the Philippine Amusement and Gaming Corp.

"PAGCOR is the sole authority that grants licenses, authorizes them, regulates them, and it’s also a gambling operator. We have to remove all of those and give back to Congress the power regulate all of these," Fariñas told ANC's Headstart.

Congress should be given such powers over the gaming industry in the same way that it has the power to grant franchises to television stations.

Fariñas was among the legislators who investigated last week's attack on Resorts World Manila, which left dozens killed after a lone suspect fired shots and set the casino floor on fire.

The lawmaker said it was clear that the resort casino, located in front of Manila's main airport, had lapses in security and was unprepared for an attack.
